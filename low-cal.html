LOW CALORIE DIET

1. Seared Salmon with Charred Green Beans

Here's what you need for this recipe:
salmon, green beans, garlic, capers and red chile. That's it!

2. Broccoli "Steaks" with Spicy Tomato Jam

Not only is Broccoli one of our favorite low-calorie foods, but it's incredibly versatile as well.
Seared in a skillet like steak and topped with crispy breadcrumbs, this is broccoli you eat with a fork and knife.

3. Air Fryer Squash Soup

Ever tried soup in an air fryer? The even heat roasts squash, carrots and aromatics perfectly before blending.

4. Sheet Pan Fish and Vegetables

Whether you're sticking to a 1,200 calorie diet plan or just trying to incorporate more fish into your dinners, this easy sheet pan fish is light, flavorful perfection.

Set out a tray of healthy shrimp lettuce wraps made with veggies and fresh herbs for dinner tonight!

6. White Bean and Kale Toasts

Not just for breakfast, toast is a totally acceptable dinner when topped with creamy beans and tender kale.

7. Garam Masala Roasted Chicken and Cauliflower

Garam masala adds warming heat and lots of flavor to this simple chicken-and-veggies meal.

8. Roasted Cauliflower Tacos

It's (vegetarian) taco night! With chili, coriander and cumin-spiced cauliflower as the filling, you won't miss the meat.

9. Best-Ever Marinated Flank Steak

LFresh orange and lime juice helps tenderize and season this steak without a ton of excess sugar or oil.

Add a sweet-sour touch to juicy pork tenderloin with an easy balsamic, honey and thyme glaze.
